Project 1
You are working on as a junior analyst  at a relocation firm.Where to purchase a new office space for their business.
All of the company s' employees will have opportuinty to move to the new area.

Table name city columns
city_name  ,avg_temp ,avg_commute , happiness_ranking 


Client requirement 
Avg_temp is between 45-60, Avg_commute is less than 60 min, Happiness ranking is top 15 cities 

SQL Query 
select city_name, avg_temp, avg_commute ,happiness_ranking
From cities 
where avg_temp between 45 and 60
and avg_commute < 60
and happiness ranking <= 15
